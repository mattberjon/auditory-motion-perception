Glossary
========

.. glossary::
  
  ASIO
    Audio Stream Input/Output.

  DAC
    Digital Analog Converter.

  HRIR
    Head Related Impulse Response.

  HRTF
    Head Related Transfer Function.

  RAM
    Random Access Memory.

  RAS
    Real Auditory Space.

  RT
    Reverberation Time.
  
  SNR
    Signal to Noise Ratio.

  VAS
    Virtual Auditory Space.
